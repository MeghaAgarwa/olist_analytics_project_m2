# olist-analytics-project-m2 #

## End-to-end analytics engineering project using the Olist e-commerce dataset with dbt, semantic modeling, and dashboards. ##

Analytics-ready reporting layer built on the Olist e-commerce dataset using dbt, semantic modeling, and Power BI.


## Target Personas

### Raj Mehta – Chief Operations Officer

**Goals:**
- Monitor business performance and revenue growth
- Compare category and regional performance
- Identify risks and review forecasts

**Key Questions:**
- How is revenue trending over time?
- Which regions and categories drive growth?
- Are there operational risks affecting performance?

**Dashboard:**
Executive Overview Dashboard

### Anjali Sharma – Business Data Analyst

**Goals:**
- Analyze trends and identify performance drivers
- Investigate operational issues
- Support leadership with insights

**Key Questions:**
- What factors impact revenue changes?
- Which segments contribute most to performance?
- Are there anomalies or delivery bottlenecks?

**Dashboard:**
Operations & Analysis Dashboard

## Persona to Dashboard Mapping

| Persona | Dashboard | Primary Metrics |
|----------|------------|------------------|
| COO | Executive Overview | Revenue, Orders, AOV, Growth %, Forecast |
| Business Analyst | Operations & Analysis | Delivery Time, Freight Cost, Delayed Orders, Anomalies |
