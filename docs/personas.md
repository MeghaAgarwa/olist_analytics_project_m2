PERSONAS 

Persona 1: Executive Leadership
Name: Raj Mehta – Chief Operations Officer (COO)

Role Summary:

Responsible for monitoring overall business health, identifying risks and opportunities, and making strategic decisions based on high-level performance indicators.

Analytics Maturity:

Consumes dashboards regularly but performs limited self-service analysis. Prefers concise, visually clear summaries.

Goals:

* Monitor revenue growth and business performance
* Track regional and category-level performance
* Identify emerging risks and operational bottlenecks
* Assess progress against historical trends
* Support strategic planning and resource allocation

Pain Points:

* Excessive metrics with unclear business impact
* Inconsistent definitions across reports
* Limited time for detailed analysis
* Difficulty identifying priority issues quickly

Key Business Questions:

* How is revenue trending over time?
* Which product categories and regions drive growth?
* How do current results compare with previous periods (WoW/MoM/YoY)?
* Are delivery performance or cancellations creating business risks?
* What does the short-term revenue forecast indicate?

Primary Metrics:

* Total Revenue
* Order Volume
* Average Order Value (AOV)
* Revenue Growth %
* Cancellation Rate
* Delayed Delivery Rate
* Forecasted Revenue



Persona 2: Business Data Analyst

Name: Anjali Sharma – Business Data Analyst

Role Summary:

Partners with business teams to investigate trends, perform root-cause analysis, and generate actionable insights.

Analytics Maturity:

Advanced user with strong analytical skills. Requires flexible filtering, drill-down capabilities, and access to detailed dimensions.

Goals:

* Identify patterns and performance drivers
* Analyze customer, seller, and product segments
* Explain changes in business metrics
* Build forecasts and monitor anomalies
* Support leadership with evidence-based recommendations

Pain Points:

* Inconsistent metric definitions
* Limited access to granular data
* Slow dashboard performance
* Difficulty tracing metrics back to source data

Key Business Questions:

* Which categories, sellers, or regions influence revenue changes?
* What factors contribute to delayed deliveries?
* Which customer segments generate the highest value?
* Are there unusual spikes or drops in orders or revenue?
* How do freight costs vary by region and category?

Primary Metrics:

* Revenue
* Orders
* Delivery Time
* Freight Cost
* Customer Lifetime Value (optional)
* Repeat Customer Rate
* Cancellation Rate
* Anomaly Count
